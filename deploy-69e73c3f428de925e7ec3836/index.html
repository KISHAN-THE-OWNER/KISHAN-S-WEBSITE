The Vision

Own Your Life,
Own Your Future

"Out of millions of people who could have found this book. Yet somehow, this specific book ended up in your hands at this exact moment. Nothing about this is random."

This book is born from a simple yet profound truth: You are the owner of your destiny. In a world that constantly tries to define who you should be, this book empowers you to choose yourself — to become the architect of your own story.

Through practical wisdom and thought-provoking insights, discover how to break free from limiting beliefs, embrace responsibility, and create the life you truly desire.

The Author

Meet Kishan Kumar Gullaiya

Kishan Kumar Gullaiya is an entrepreneur, author, and a firm believer in the ownership mindset. His journey from navigating life's challenges to building multiple ventures has given him unique insights into what it truly means to take ownership of one's destiny.

Through his writing, content creation, and business endeavors, Kishan aims to inspire others to embrace personal responsibility and create lives of meaning and purpose. "The Owner: I Choose You" distills years of experience, reflection, and learning into actionable wisdom.
